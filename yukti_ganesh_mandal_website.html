About Yukti Ganesh Mandal

"Yukti" signifies wisdom, strategy and togetherness. Shree Yukti Ganesh Mandal brings the local community together to celebrate devotion and culture. Our mandal aims to spread joy, preserve tradition, and support local members through the festival.

Events & Updates

  • 28–03 Sept — Daily Aarti & Bhajan: Morning 7:00 AM | Evening 7:30 PM
  • 04 Sep — Visarjan: Procession starts at 3:00 PM
